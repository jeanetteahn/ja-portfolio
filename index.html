About

Hi, I'm Jeanette, a graduate student at the University of Michigan specializing in User Experience Design and Research. I explore how design can reveal hidden connections, between people, ideas, and everyday systems, by creating digital experiences that are both thoughtful and intuitive. My work blends perspectives from museums, marketing, and healthcare to bring overlooked stories to life.
